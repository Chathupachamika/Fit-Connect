About BMI

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a measure of body fat based on height and weight that applies to adult men and women. BMI is a useful tool for identifying potential weight problems, but it's not a diagnostic tool.

A healthcare provider can help you understand your BMI results and determine if you need to make any changes to your lifestyle.
